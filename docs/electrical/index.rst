Electrical
=============

Electrical types and capabilities for power plugs, electrical outlets and
sockets and more. The most common type is :doc:`power-outlet <outlets>` which is
used to represent a single generic outlet/socket. Such a power outlet may be a
child of other types such as the individual outlets in a
:doc:`power strip <strips>` or a :doc:`wall outlet <wall-outlets>`.
